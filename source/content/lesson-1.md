**Part 1: “Zoom Out” on *what* you need to focus on** We’ll take a high level and strategic view of your life, as if you’re a photographer zooming out to get the big picture. We'll help you map out your life’s purpose, define goals, and gain a deeper understanding of what you really want—and what’s holding you back.

**Part 2: “Zoom In” on how you will achieve your goals** We’ll help you define your life’s goals and cover the details on how to make your goals happen, while providing you with scientifically proven methods to ensure you achieve them in record time. You’ll discover simple methods to improve your focus, develop—and maintain—good habits, block distractions, and follow through on your goals.
