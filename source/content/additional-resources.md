The following resources are shared through Google Docs or Trello.

## To create a free Trello account:

 	
- Go to [Trello.com](http://trello.com/)
 	
- Click "Sign Up"
 	
- Enter your name, email, and password
 	
- Click "Create New Account"

Note:

 	
- Google Docs are view only.

 	
  - To copy content: Select Edit >> Copy
 	
  - To download content: Select File >> Download

 	
- Trello boards are view only.  You may copy the board.

 	
  - Log in to [Trello.com](https://trello.com/)
 	
  - Open the board you want to copy (links provided below)
 	
  - Click Show Menu >> More >> Copy Board
 	
  - Enter a new title (ensure "Keep Cards" is check-marked)

Here’s a [step-by-step walk through](http://help.trello.com/article/806-moving-cards-or-lists) explaining how to move cards in Trello. Got it?  Let's get into the resources...
